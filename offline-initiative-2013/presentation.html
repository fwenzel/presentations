Challenges for Offline-Capable Web Apps

Fred Wenzel, Mozilla

What does "offline-capable app" mean?

  • After installing, the app "exists" on your device
  • You can open it regardless of your network status
  • It does something useful (whatever that means for your app)
  • Catches up when reconnecting
  • Example: Email app

Why do we care?

  • Many of our users in places without great data connections
    • (or commuting on Highway 237)
  • No/spotty access = a part of life; normal state
  • Data = expensive
  • Though everywhere in the world, users care about performance
  • Offline is not an edge case
(source: Mozilla User Research)
